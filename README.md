Legal Document RAG Assistant 🏛️⚖️
An innovative Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system specifically designed for legal document analysis and consultation. This system combines advanced NLP techniques with legal domain expertise to provide intelligent legal insights from contracts, regulations, case law, and other legal documents.
🚀 Key Innovations

Legal-Specific Text Processing: Custom preprocessing that preserves legal terminology and extracts legal entities (case citations, statute references, contract clauses)
Multi-Jurisdictional Support: Handle documents from different legal jurisdictions (US, EU, etc.)
Practice Area Classification: Organize documents by legal practice areas (corporate, litigation, IP, employment, etc.)
Query Type Specialization: Different response formats for research, compliance, drafting, and analysis queries
Legal Entity Extraction: Automatically identify and extract legal references, dollar amounts, dates, and citations
Risk Assessment Integration: Provides risk analysis and compliance recommendations

🏗️ Architecture
Legal RAG System
├── LegalTextProcessor     # Advanced legal text preprocessing
├── DocumentParser         # Multi-format document parsing (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
├── LegalKnowledgeBase    # Vector database with legal metadata
└── LegalRAGSystem        # Main orchestration and query processing
✨ Features
Document Processing

Multi-format Support: PDF, DOCX, TXT, and Markdown files
Legal Entity Recognition: Automatic extraction of case citations, statute references, contract clauses
Key Term Extraction: TF-IDF based identification of important legal terms
Automatic Summarization: Generate concise summaries of legal documents

Intelligent Querying

Practice Area Filtering: Search within specific legal domains
Jurisdictional Filtering: Focus on relevant legal jurisdictions
Query Type Specialization:

Research: General legal research with key legal considerations
Compliance: Compliance analysis with recommendations
Drafting: Document drafting guidance with suggested clauses
Analysis: Legal analysis with risk assessment



Advanced Analytics

Document Clustering: Group similar legal documents automatically
Similarity Search: Find the most relevant documents using cosine similarity
Confidence Scoring: Assess the relevance of search results
Usage Statistics: Track document types, practice areas, and system usage
